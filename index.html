Bell Pepper Plant

The plant is a tropical perennial, but it is usually grown as an annual vegetable. The plant grows upright and bushy. They have dark green lance-shaped leaves, small white flowers, and edible fruits. The fruit is classified as a berry and ranges in color from green, yellow, orange, red, purple, black, and white.
